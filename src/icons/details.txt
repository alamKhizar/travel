Firstly, the user will sign up and enter their details to register themselves on TMS, then the user will login to the system and start the procedure from the very first step that is to add details where the system will take your CNIC, address and other details and these details can also be viewed and edited as well.  They will be able to select their desired destinations for their trip and the system will offer different packages according to the selected destination which includes hotel booking and visits to the famous places. Users can also calculate the estimated amount by the calculator integrated in the system and then confirm the desired trip by paying the total amount.
